Meet The Team

Draven Nightshade

Head Caretaker

Draven, a mysterious and reclusive figure, possesses a deep connection with mystical creatures, nurturing them with a unique blend of ancient remedies and modern medicine.

Elara Moonshadow

Animal Behavior Specialist

Elara is an empathic communicator, able to decipher the complex emotions of mythical creatures and help them heal their psychological wounds.

Magnus Ironheart

Magical Healer

Magnus, a grizzled mage, uses his arcane knowledge to mend the most intricate and mystical of injuries, often crafting enchanted prosthetics for the creatures in need.

Seraphina Stormrider

Environmental Enrichment Coordinator

Seraphina creates magical habitats for the creatures, ensuring they feel at home and experience a smooth transition into rehabilitation.

Orik Fireforge

Head of Security

A dwarf with an unshakable sense of duty, Orik keeps both the creatures and the staff safe, specializing in warding off any potential threats.

Celestia Frostwind

Mythical Historian

Celestia is an expert in the lore and legends surrounding mythical creatures, helping to understand their cultural and historical context.
